Sources

January 11, 2017

By Micah

Brake Bread is a local bakery and we build on this by supporting local producers and suppliers.
Swany White – Freeport MN

  • Wheat (Our white bread flour)
  • Organic Whole Wheat
  • Organic Rye
  • Organic Rolled Oats
  • Organic Steel Cut Oats

Whole Grain Milling – Welcome MN

  • Organic Buckwheat
  • Organic Sesame Seeds
  • Organic Sunflower Seeds
  • Organic Hi Lysine Cornmeal
  • Organic Brown Flax
  • Organic Whole Wheat Pastry Flour

Baker’s Field Flour – Mpls MN

  • Organic Whole Wheat and Sifted Whole Wheat

Bootstrap Coffee Roaster – St. Paul MN

  • Coffee. Many Fair Trade and Organic

Stony Creek Dairy – Melrose MN

  • Vat Pasteurized Low Temp Whole Milk
  • Butter
  • Heavy Cream

Lots of little odds and ends (spices, baking soda, stuff not grown locally like coconut) come from Restaurant Depot, Mississippi Market and Kortes. If you have suggestions for more local sources we should work with please drop us a line at [email protected].
